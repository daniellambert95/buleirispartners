What This Session Is About

Running a business is hard enough — understanding the numbers shouldn't make it harder.

This session is designed for business owners who want clarity, not confusion. You'll understand what numbers to focus on (which aren't in your accounts), why cash feels tight even when sales are good, and the simple steps every SME can take to build stronger, more predictable financial results.

If you've ever felt unsure about where the money goes… this session is for you.
