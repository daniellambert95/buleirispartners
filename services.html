Profit & Cash Flow Targets

In our initial kickoff meeting, we set clear, ambitious, and realistic financial targets for your business — across revenue, profit, and cash flow.

This gives us a clear destination.

Business Assessment

Then, each month, we'll analyze your business and numbers to determine how well your business is performing.

Forecast Your Figures

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Reliable bookkeeping is the foundation of every successful business. We keep your financial records accurate, organised, and up to date so you always know where your business stands.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Accurate recording of income, expenses, supplier payments, and customer receipts to ensure your accounts stay current each month.

Reconciliations

Regular reconciliation of bank accounts, credit cards, loans, and key balances to identify discrepancies early and maintain accuracy.

Clean Month-End Close

Structured monthly close process to finalise accounts promptly, ensuring reliable reports and no backlog of unfinished entries.

Accounts Organisation

Clear and professional organisation of your accounting records, ledgers, and documentation for easier management and future growth.

Reporting & Financial Statements

Strong reporting gives business owners clarity, confidence, and better decision-making. We turn raw numbers into useful financial insight.

Monthly Management Reports

Easy-to-understand monthly reports highlighting revenue, costs, margins, cash flow, and overall business performance.

Profit & Loss Statements

Clear income statements showing where profit is being made, where margins are slipping, and where action may be needed.

Balance Sheet

Accurate overview of your assets, liabilities, and equity to understand the true financial strength of the business.

Cash Flow Reporting

Insight into money coming in and going out, helping you manage liquidity and avoid unnecessary cash pressure.

Annual Financial Statement Preparation

Professionally prepared year-end financial statements ready for management review, lenders, tax advisors, or auditors.
