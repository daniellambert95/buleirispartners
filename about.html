About Blue Iris Partners

Practical financial leadership, built on real-world experience.

Blue Iris Partners was founded to bring high-level financial expertise - typically reserved for large corporates and institutional clients - to growing businesses.

Our experience includes working with the big four as well as working on complex financial structures, managing multi-billion-dollar portfolios, and supporting multinational groups across industries including real estate, hospitality, and financial services.

We have worked closely with boards, investors, auditors, and stakeholders - delivering financial reporting, analysis, and decision support in high-pressure, high-value environments.

That same level of discipline, structure, and insight is now applied to small and mid-sized businesses.

We combine CFO strategy with dependable finance operations - from bookkeeping and reporting to tax coordination, audit readiness, and transaction support - giving you one trusted partner overseeing your entire financial function.

Clearer numbers. Better decisions.
Stronger, more profitable businesses.
